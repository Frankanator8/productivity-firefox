## [0.1.0] - 2026-05-11

### Added
- Initial release.
- Instagram: hide "Suggested for you" / "Suggested posts" feed articles, right-rail
  user list, `/explore/` and `/reels/` main content, and profile-page "Suggested
  for you" carousel.
- Reddit: hide recommendation post cards, "Recommended Communities" / "Trending
  today" / "Communities you might like" widgets, "More posts you may like" under
  comment threads, and `/r/popular` + `/r/all` listings.
- Popup: 10-problem two-digit-addition gauntlet, 1/5/10/15 min unblock windows,
  Lock-now button, live time-remaining countdown.
- Background: 15-minute hard cap on unblock duration; live broadcast of state
  changes to open Instagram/Reddit tabs (no reload required).
